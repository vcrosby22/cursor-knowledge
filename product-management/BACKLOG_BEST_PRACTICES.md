## Layering work (generic pattern)

Many teams structure work as **outcome → theme → item**:

- **Outcome / north star** — why a strand of work exists.
- **Theme or epic** — a bucket of related work under that outcome.
- **Story or task** — a concrete, deliverable slice (often with optional long-form description or acceptance notes elsewhere).

Exact names and IDs vary by org. The URLs below apply regardless of tool.

## Applying the ideas

Prioritize and refine the **top** of the backlog in line with the Scrum Guide and Pichler-style guidance: **clear near-term items**, **coarse** far-term items until they are next.
